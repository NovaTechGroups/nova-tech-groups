Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At TaskBar, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a secure and rewarding experience, we collect the following data:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up via Email or Google Sign-In, we collect your Email Address and Username to create and manage your account.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect your Android ID to prevent fraud, ensure one account per device, and maintain the integrity of our reward system.
  • Usage Data: We track your "Bolts" (points) balance, task completion history, and ad engagement to calculate your rewards accurately.
  • Tracking Technologies: Our third-party partners may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to measure engagement, verify offer completions, and deliver relevant advertisements.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and allow you to access your account across devices.
  • To process reward redemptions and conduct security audits.
  • To prevent "Suspicious Activity," such as the use of VPNs, emulators, or multiple accounts on a single device.
  • To communicate important updates regarding your account or the service.
3. Third-Party Services

TaskBar integrates third-party SDKs to provide tasks and advertisements. These partners may collect their own data (such as IP addresses or Advertising IDs) according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services / Firebase: Used for authentication, database management, and analytics.
  • AdMob / PubScale: Used for delivering advertisements and tracking offer completions.
  • Our third-party partners may also use cookies or similar tracking technologies to measure engagement and deliver relevant advertisements.
4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information as long as your account is active.

Data Deletion: If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at info.taskbar@gmail.com. Once processed, your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including Firebase's secure authentication and encrypted database connections, to protect your data from unauthorized access.

6. Children's Privacy

TaskBar is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user personal information. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers such as Google Firebase, AdMob, and offerwall partners for the purpose of operating the service.
